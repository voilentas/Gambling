Games & Gameplay
How does Coinflip work?
Coinflip is a head-to-head game where two sides stake items/credit. The winner is chosen by a provably fair roll, and the winner receives the combined pot (minus any applicable fees).
How does Jackpot work?
Jackpot is a multiplayer pot. Players deposit items/credit into a round; the more value you contribute, the higher your chance to win. At the end of the round, one winner is selected to receive the pot.
How does the Upgrader work?
Upgrader lets you risk an item/amount for a chance to “upgrade” into a higher value target. You set the win chance (or select a target), then the provably fair system determines the result.
Are there game limits (min/max)?
Most game modes have minimum and maximum deposit limits to keep games fair and prevent abuse. Limits can vary by mode and may change over time.

Provably Fair System
What does “provably fair” mean?
“Provably fair” means you can verify the outcome of a game using cryptographic values (like seeds and nonces). This makes it possible to confirm that results were not changed after you joined a game.
How do I verify a result?
Open the “Provably Fair” panel (usually near the game controls) and follow the verification steps shown there. You’ll compare the revealed server seed against its hash and reproduce the roll using the client seed and nonce.
Can the site change the outcome?
With a properly implemented provably fair setup, the server seed is committed (hashed) before play, and only revealed after—making tampering detectable when you verify.
